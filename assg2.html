Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiling-hot water
  • 3 tablespoons instant-espresso powder
  • 1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon sugar, divided
  • 3 tablespoons Tia Maria (coffee liqueur)
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup dry Marsala
  • 1 pound mascarpone (2 1/2 cups)
  • 1 cup chilled heavy cream
  • 36 savoiardi (crisp Italian ladyfingers; from two 7-ounce packages)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder for dusting

Preparation

  1. Mix water, espresso powder, 1 tbsp of sugar, and Tia Maria until sugar dissolve then cool.
  2. You’ll need sauce pan of barely simmering water. Egg yolks, Marsala, and ½ cup of sugar in a metal bowl set over saucepan. Remove bowl from heat. Mix mascarpone together.
  3. Beat cream in large bowl.
  4. Fold mascarpone mixture into whipped cream.
  5. Dipping ladyfingers into coffee mixture. Line bottom in cake pan with 18 ladyfingers in 3 row.
  6. Spread half of mascarpone filling on top.
  7. Dip remaining 18 ladyfingers in coffee and arrange over filling in pan, spread remaining mascarpone filling on top and dust with cocoa.
  8. Chill, covered, at least 6 hours. Let the tiramisu in room temperature 30 mins before serving.
